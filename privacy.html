Privacy Policy — One More Tap

Decanus LtdLast updated: 28 December 2025

1. Introduction

One More Tap (“the App”) is a casual mobile game developed by Decanus Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, why it is collected, and how it is used.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not require accounts and does not ask for personal profile information.

2a) Gameplay data

  • High scores and leaderboard entries
  • Game mode played (e.g. normal / insane)

This is used only to power leaderboards and to improve gameplay balance.

2b) Device & usage data

The App may process limited device and usage information automatically via third-party services, such as:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App performance information and crash logs
  • Approximate location (non-precise, such as country/region level) for advertising purposes

3. Advertising

One More Tap uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process information such as advertising identifiers, ad interaction data, and signals used to provide ads.

Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Analytics & Leaderboards

The App uses Firebase services for leaderboards and basic analytics (such as performance and crash reporting). We do not use Firebase to collect personal profiles or require sign-in.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Data Storage & Security

Data processed by the App is stored and handled by trusted third-party platforms (such as Google Firebase and Google AdMob). We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed by the App, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights & Controls

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request deletion or access to information. Because the App does not use accounts, we do not store identifiable user profiles.

You can also limit ad personalisation using your device settings (where available).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
